About

The Cybersecurity Week comes at a time where the public opinion about this topic is divided. Our student branch aims to offer the University of Porto's vast community an event that promotes the learning of different topics in the diverse areas of Cybersecurity.

During the week, participants will be able to take part in lectures, workshops and socializing and networking sessions, informally contancting directly with specialists in the various sub-areas, whether technical, soft skills or curiosity, which are not covered in the curricular plan nor are they approached easily and so openly.

When

11-15 March 2019

Where

Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto
